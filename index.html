Good To Know

Frequently Asked

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes — your first session is on us. Walk in during opening hours or book ahead using the form below, and a coach will show you around.

Do I need any experience to join?

Not at all. Inshape is beginner-friendly — our certified trainers guide every movement with proper form so you feel confident from day one.

What are your timings?

We're open Monday to Saturday, 5:30 AM – 10:00 PM, with a dedicated ladies' batch from 12:00–3:00 PM, and Sunday mornings 6:00–11:00 AM.

Do you provide diet plans and personal training?

Yes. Custom diet plans come with our Pro and Elite memberships, and one-on-one personal training is available as part of Elite or as an add-on.

Can I freeze or pause my membership?

Annual (Elite) members can freeze their membership when travelling or unwell — just let the front desk know in advance.

Is parking available?

Yes, we offer free parking, and the whole facility is air-conditioned with clean changing rooms and showers.
